Module starting date:

These dates only apply to 10-credit modules. The 40-credit modules have different arrangements.

Submission dates for assessed work should normally be 10:00 am on a Monday morning, unless holidays intervene.

Submission dates are five weeks after a module has finished for full-time students and eight weeks for flexible. Extra weeks are added for Christmas and Easter vacations.

Feedback dates are 15 working days after submission.
